
# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

## Project
Healthcare LMS Analytics Platform

## Objective

The objective of this project is to provide management with actionable insights into student performance, course performance, revenue, marketing effectiveness, and operational efficiency.

---

# 1. Student KPIs

### Total Students
Definition:
Total number of registered students.

Business Value:
Measures overall platform growth.

---

### Active Students
Definition:
Students who logged into the LMS within the last 30 days.

Business Value:
Indicates learner engagement.

---

### Course Completion Rate

Formula:

Completed Students / Total Enrolled Students × 100

Business Value:
Measures course effectiveness.

---

### Student Retention Rate

Formula:

Returning Students / Total Students × 100

Business Value:
Measures long-term engagement.

---

# 2. Enrollment KPIs

### Total Enrollments

Definition:
Total course enrollments.

---

### Monthly Enrollments

Definition:
New enrollments each month.

---

### Enrollment Growth %

Formula:

(Current Month - Previous Month)
/ Previous Month ×100

---

# 3. Revenue KPIs

### Total Revenue

Definition:
Total revenue generated from enrollments.

---

### Revenue Per Course

Definition:
Revenue generated by each course.

---

### Average Revenue Per Student

Formula:

Total Revenue / Total Students

---

# 4. Marketing KPIs

### Lead Conversion Rate

Formula:

Enrollments / Leads ×100

---

### Campaign ROI

Formula:

(Revenue - Campaign Cost)
/ Campaign Cost ×100

---

### Cost Per Enrollment

Formula:

Campaign Cost / Total Enrollments

---

# 5. Learning KPIs

### Average Quiz Score

Definition:
Average score achieved by students.

---

### Average Progress %

Definition:
Average course completion percentage.

---

### Average Watch Time

Definition:
Average learning hours spent on the LMS.

---

# 6. Course KPIs

### Top Performing Courses

Measured By:

- Revenue
- Enrollment
- Completion Rate

---

### Least Performing Courses

Measured By:

- Low Enrollment
- Low Completion
- Low Revenue

---

# 7. Operational KPIs

Definition:
Completion rate of each batch.

---

### Instructor Performance

Measured By:
