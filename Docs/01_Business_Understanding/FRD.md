
# Functional Requirements Document (FRD)

## Healthcare LMS Analytics Platform

Version: 1.0

Author: Sunal Singh

Project Type:
Independent Portfolio Project inspired by real Healthcare Education workflows.

## Objectives

The Healthcare LMS Analytics Platform aims to:

- Centralize LMS reporting
- Improve business visibility
- Enable data-driven decision making
- Provide trusted KPIs
- Support stakeholders through interactive dashboards

## Functional Requirements

### FR-001 Data Collection

The platform shall collect data from:

- Student Management
- Course Management
- Marketing Campaigns
- Assessments
- Finance
- Learning Activity

### FR-002 Bronze Layer

The platform shall store incoming raw datasets in the Bronze Layer without modification.

### FR-003 Silver Layer

The platform shall standardize, cleanse and validate incoming datasets.

### FR-004 Gold Layer

The platform shall create business-ready analytical models using Star Schema.
